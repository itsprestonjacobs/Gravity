What is Gravity?

Gravity is the force that pulls objects toward each other. It is what keeps the planets in orbit around the Sun and makes objects fall to the ground.

Planets in the Solar System

Earth

The third planet from the Sun, home to life, and a perfect example of gravity at work.

Jupiter

The largest planet in the Solar System, with a massive gravitational pull.

Saturn

Famous for its rings, Saturn's gravity affects many moons and space objects.
